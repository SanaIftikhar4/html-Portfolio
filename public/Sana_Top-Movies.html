My Favorite Movies

My top 3 favorite movies

  1. The GREATEST SHOWMAN

    "The Greatest Showman" is a movie about P.T. Barnum, who started a famous circus. It's known for its catchy songs and shows how people with different talents come together to create something special.

  2. TOP GUN

    "Top Gun" is a classic action film from 1986 starring Tom Cruise as Maverick, a daring fighter pilot. The movie is packed with thrilling aerial scenes and follows Maverick's journey as he trains at the elite Top Gun school. It's known for its high-flying action and memorable moments.

  3. AVENGERS END GAME

    "Avengers: Endgame" is a thrilling superhero film where the heroes team up to battle the powerful villain, Thanos. It's packed with exciting action and memorable moments that make it a must-watch for fans of the Marvel universe.
